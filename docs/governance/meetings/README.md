# Governance meeting records

Language Design Committee meetings are preferably focused offline or
synchronous sessions. This directory makes their design work durable and
public without requiring a transcript.

## Before a meeting

Copy [0000-template.md](0000-template.md) to a file named YYYY-MM-DD.md. If
more than one committee meeting occurs on a date, add a short suffix. Publish
the agenda and linked pre-reading at least 48 hours beforehand when
practicable.

An agenda item seeking a formal decision must identify the issue or RFD,
requested disposition, known conflicts, and material that participants should
read. An unannounced item may be discussed, but normally waits for a properly
noticed decision.

## During and after

The record must distinguish:

- evidence and alternatives considered;
- unresolved questions;
- non-binding conclusions;
- formal decisions;
- votes, abstentions, recusals, and quorum;
- use of the Design Steward role; and
- actions with owners and target dates.

Publish notes within seven calendar days. Attendees may propose corrections,
but the Secretary is responsible for an accurate record. A recording or
verbatim transcript is neither required nor presumed.

The public note may link to an RFD decision record rather than duplicate it.
A language decision is binding only when the written disposition and rationale
are published in the repository.

## Privacy and redaction

Do not put conduct reports, reporter identities, private personnel matters,
embargoed security details, personal contact information, or legal advice in a
meeting note. If a private matter affects public operations, record only the
minimum safe fact, such as “a member recused for a confidential conflict.”

The public-record rule exists for design accountability. It never overrides
the [Code of Conduct](../../../CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md), a person's safety, or a
legitimate confidentiality duty.

## Working-group records

Each WG keeps its own meeting notes inside its numbered directory because
those records belong to the study. WG notes use the template in the
[working-group template](../../working-groups/0000-template/meetings/YYYY-MM-DD.md)
and remain non-normative.
